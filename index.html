The Basics:

The first thing you need to learn about cooking is safety. If you are not safe cooking can be very dangerous if you don't know how to clean or hold a knife correctly. Here are some general safety tips about cooking.

  • When frying, grilling, boiling or broiling food,always stay inside of the kitchen and watch carefully.
  • Make sure flammables are away from anything can can create fire, such as a stove top.
  • When using oil make sure you add the food gently to the pot, so the oil doesn't splatter.
  • Make sure you keep an eye on what you fry. If you see wisps of smoke or the oil smells,immediately turn off the burner and/or carefully remove the pan from the burner. Smoke is a danger sign that the oil is too hot.
